Inception

Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from people's dreams. He is given a chance to erase his criminal past by planting an idea into someone's mind, a process known as "inception".

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, and Tom Hardy as Eames.
